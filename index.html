About Us

Gelato e Caffè is a contemporary Italian caffé showcasing techniques rooted in Italy, carefully sourcing only the best ingredients. Building off the vibrant food scene of Italy, Gelato e Caffè displays an inspiring take on Italian cuisine in a relaxing environment. This enables our genuine approach to food & hospitality to shine. Gelato e Caffè was recognized with one star in the 2020, 2021 & 2022 Michelin guides.
